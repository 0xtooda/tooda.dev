FAQ

Questions people always ask

How long does it take to build my site?+
Depends on the package — a landing page takes 5 days, a full multi-page site around 10 days, and a web app up to 3 weeks. I'll always give you a clear timeline before we start.
Do I own the code when it's done?+
100%. Once the project is paid and delivered, the code is fully yours. No licensing, no ongoing fees, no strings attached.
What do you need from me to get started?+
Just your idea, any branding you have (logo, colors, fonts), and examples of sites you like. I'll handle the rest and ask if I need anything else along the way.
Why are you cheaper than Fiverr?+
On Fiverr you're paying for the platform, middlemen, and often getting a template with your logo slapped on it. I build everything from scratch, you talk directly to me, and you get Ronday Agency-quality work for half the price.
Can you redesign my existing website?+
Absolutely. Send me a link to your current site and tell me what's not working. I'll rebuild it from the ground up — faster, cleaner, and something you're actually proud of.
Do you offer payment plans?+
Yes. For bigger projects we can split it — 50% upfront to start, 50% on delivery. Just mention it when you reach out and we'll work something out.
